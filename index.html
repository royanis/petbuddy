Everything You Need For Complete Pet Care

PetBuddy combines all essential pet care tools in one intuitive app. From health tracking to memory preservation, we've got you covered.

Health Tracking

Keep comprehensive health records, track vaccinations, medications, and vet visits. Get automated reminders for upcoming appointments.

Growth Charts

Monitor weight, height, and other vital measurements with visual charts. Compare growth against breed standards and track progress.

Smart Reminders

Never miss important care tasks with customizable notifications. Set reminders for medications, grooming, and routine check-ups.

Activity Logging

Track daily activities like walks, meals, and playtime. Perfect for households with multiple caregivers or pet sitters.

Pet Journal

Create a digital scrapbook with photos, videos, and milestone memories. Document your pet's journey from puppy to senior.

Private & Secure

All data is stored locally on your device. No cloud uploads, no privacy concerns. Your pet's information stays completely private.
