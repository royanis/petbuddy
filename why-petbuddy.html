Common Questions

Get answers to frequently asked questions about PetBuddy:

Is PetBuddy really free?

Yes! PetBuddy is completely free to download and use. All core features including health tracking, reminders, growth charts, and pet journal are included at no cost. We display non-intrusive ads to support development.

How is my data kept private?

PetBuddy stores all data locally on your device. We don't upload your pet's information to any servers or cloud services. You have complete control over your data, and it remains private to you.

Can I use PetBuddy for multiple pets?

Currently, PetBuddy supports one pet profile per app. We're actively developing multi-pet support, which will be available in a future update. This will allow separate profiles for each of your pets.

What makes PetBuddy different from other pet apps?

PetBuddy prioritizes privacy (100% local data storage), works completely offline, and provides comprehensive features in one app. Unlike others, we don't collect or sell your data, and all core features are free.

Can I backup my pet's data?

Yes! You can create local backups of your journal entries and images. Export your data to your device's storage and import it back when needed. We're expanding backup capabilities in future updates.

Is PetBuddy available for Android?

PetBuddy is currently available for iOS devices. We're working on the Android version, which will be released soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when the Android app launches.
