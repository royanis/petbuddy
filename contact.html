Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about PetBuddy

How is my pet's data stored and protected?

PetBuddy stores all your pet's information locally on your device, not on our servers. This means you have complete control over your data, and it remains private to you. We implement industry-standard security practices to ensure the app itself is secure.

Can I use PetBuddy for multiple pets?

Currently, PetBuddy supports managing a single pet. We are actively working on adding multi-pet support, which will be included in a future release. This upcoming feature will allow you to create individual profiles, records, and journals for each of your furry family members.

Is PetBuddy available for both iOS and Android?

Yes, PetBuddy is available on both the Apple App Store for iOS devices and Google Play Store for Android devices. The app features and functionality are consistent across both platforms.

How do I backup my pet's data?

Currently, PetBuddy allows you to backup and restore only your journal entries and the images within those journals. Navigate to Settings > Backup & Restore to create a local backup file that you can store on your device. A more comprehensive backup solution, including cloud storage options for all pet data, is planned for a future release.

Is there a subscription fee for using PetBuddy?

PetBuddy is available as a free download with all core features included. We display non-intrusive ads to support the app's development. We may offer premium features in the future as optional in-app purchases, but the essential functionality will always remain free.
