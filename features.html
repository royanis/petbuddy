Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about PetBuddy's features and functionality.

Can I use PetBuddy for multiple pets?

Currently, PetBuddy supports one pet profile per app installation. We're actively developing multi-pet support, which will be available in a future update. This feature will allow you to create separate profiles for each pet with individual health records, journals, and reminders.

How does the offline functionality work?

PetBuddy stores all data locally on your device, so you can access and update your pet's information anywhere, anytime - even without an internet connection. This means you can log activities, view health records, and check reminders whether you're at the vet, traveling, or in areas with poor connectivity.

Can I backup my pet's data?

Yes! PetBuddy allows you to create local backups of your journal entries and associated images. You can export this data to your device's storage and import it back if needed. We're working on expanding backup capabilities to include all app data in future updates.

How accurate are the growth charts?

PetBuddy's growth charts are based on general breed standards and veterinary guidelines. While they provide helpful benchmarks, every pet is unique. Always consult with your veterinarian for personalized growth assessments and health advice specific to your pet.

Can I share my pet's information with my vet?

Absolutely! You can export health records, growth charts, and activity logs to share with your veterinarian. This feature is particularly useful for appointments, emergencies, or when switching to a new vet. The exported data can be shared via email or messaging apps.
