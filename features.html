PetBuddy Features

Discover all the tools that make PetBuddy the ultimate companion app for pet owners.

Comprehensive Health Tracking

Keep all your pet's important health information organized and easily accessible. Track vaccinations, medications, and vet visits to ensure your pet receives the best care possible.

  • Record vaccinations and set renewal reminders
  • Track medications and dosage schedules
  • Store vet visit records and prescriptions
  • Monitor diet and nutrition details
  • Receive alerts for upcoming health needs

Never miss an important health appointment again. PetBuddy's detailed health tracking ensures your pet stays protected and healthy year-round.

Interactive Growth Charts

Monitor your pet's physical development with detailed growth charts and analysis. Track weight, height, and other measurements over time to ensure healthy development.

  • Visual charts showing growth progression
  • Compare against breed standards
  • Calculate growth rates and projections
  • Track multiple measurements
  • Export data for vet consultations

Make informed decisions about your pet's nutrition and exercise needs based on accurate growth data and trends.

Smart Reminders System

Stay on top of all your pet care responsibilities with customizable reminders. From medication schedules to grooming appointments, never forget an important pet care task.

  • Create one-time or recurring reminders
  • Categorize by health, grooming, or custom types
  • Set flexible notification schedules
  • Calendar view of all upcoming events
  • Mark favorites for important reminders

PetBuddy's reminder system works like your personal pet care assistant, ensuring you never miss an important task or appointment.

Daily Care Activity Logging

Easily record and track all your daily pet care activities in one convenient place. Monitor feeding, walks, medications and more to ensure consistent care.

  • Quick logging of common activities
  • Track feeding times and portions
  • Record walk duration and distance
  • Log medication administration
  • View activity statistics and trends

Perfect for households with multiple caretakers or pet sitters. Everyone stays informed about your pet's care routine and recent activities.

Pet Journal & Memories

Create a beautiful digital record of your pet's life journey. Capture special moments, milestones, and everyday adventures with photos, videos, and journal entries.

  • Create multimedia journal entries
  • Organize entries with customizable tags
  • Search and filter entries by date or category
  • Calendar view to see your pet's history
  • Safe and private storage of all memories

From first days to special achievements, create a lasting memory book that celebrates your unique bond with your pet.
